About DAMAC

DAMAC Properties has been at the forefront of the Middle East's luxury real estate market sincle 2002, delivering award-winning residential, commercial and leisure properties across the region, including the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Jordan, Lebanon, Oman and the United Kingdom.
